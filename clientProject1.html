Digio + Uber

WHITELABEL

Uber is a platform that allows those who drive and deliver to connect with riders, eaters, and restaurants. The company was searching for new ways to bring more benefits to their drivers in Brazil. They acquired the creation of a Uber Account partnering with Digio.

Problem

Build a whitelabel so Digio could sell it for Uber and other brands within a minimal time to market.

Challenges

To understand the business model to build efficient and innovative experiences. 2 months to build a whitelabel account.

My role

Make a strategy to deliver the project as fast as possible. Responsible for experience, interaction and interface.

Strategy

We organized a design sprint to visualize the steps and create a concise strategy. We relied on one of the design thinking tool as a base to work on and lead the project: The double diamond.

Discover

Firstly, we needed to find what is important for the drivers when dealing with their earnings. So we started with 2 benchmarks: Direct competitors (driving apps) and indirect competitors (Banks and fintech).

Benchmark of services similar to uber

Benchmark of bank services

Then we were able to see the must-haves for a MPV and what are the growth possibilities for the features. We proceeded with desk research as a support.

But what more can we offer to the Driver?

Anlyzing all deskresearch and benchmarking, it was possible to find a possible benefit: Instant payment for the driver.

The Uber driver earned their ride values only once a week, meaning they had to wait at least 6 days to use their earns. With the idea of instant payment, as soon as the driver finished their ride, he would have access to his money and spend it as well.

None of the products in the research showed this kind of benefit. Also, the apps didn't have an account where they could control and make transactions all in one place.

The Design team was divided into squads along with POs, Developers, and Q.A.s.

I focused on two squads: “First steps” and “Payments” where I was responsible for all acquisition and payment flows of Uber Account.

Develop

We created all core tokens based on references taken out of the Uber app. All iconic and visual parts were also built with social media, sites, and apps references.

Components with Uber tokens

Components ready to be coded

That out of the way, we could finally start to develop. Thinking about the listed features, we looked over Digio inspecting all flows to decide whether changes would be needed, based on data previously collected.

First steps squad

It was necessary to make some adjustments on the subscription flow for some reasons:

1. The process would happen in webview, differently then in Digio, that does it directly on Digio.

2. As a bank and a drivers app we had some different informations to comply with the user, we needed to do some extra validations behind the scenes.

Uber Userflow

With that, we had more freedom to explore more visual resources than a pure whitelabel, and we did some thematizations.

Payments squad

As we mainted most of features, nothing changeded besides dapplying the Uber tokens. But all funcionalities remained the same as Digio.

Deliver

Everything ordered on the design aspect (flows corrected, interfaces ready...) we sent user flows to tag and started to organize a user test for future improvements and study possibilities.

QA testes were made and bugs and inconsistencies found were appointed in Jira. After every correction was made, we tested again for a double-check.
